We, also a humanitarian mission, accept donations. Please, if you can and believe in the potential of this mission, kindly donate here.
With this project, which uses current technology, every 1200 € provides lifelong food for a person's life.
This value corresponds to the calculation for 3 doses of soybean sweet potato soup per day for each person.
Please donate what you can.
Your donation is an investment and will be used for research and construction of autonomous agricultural machinery. The goal is to reach the poorest countries while spreading commercially fund-raising machines, which will be spread across richer countries. Who has no money does not pay.
