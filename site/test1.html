Technology and method to fight hunger:

We introduce machines the size of a 20 and 40 foot shipping container that produces food everyday.

What each machine of strawberries does:

Produces 800kg / 1764 pounds of strawberries each year.

What each machine of sweet potatoes does:

Every hour, from 9:00h to 15:00h, up to 7 hot baked sweet potatoes are served by each machine, totaling up to 40 every day.

The sweet potato is the food that produces the most carbohydrates per square meter.

About the machines:

The machines are fundamentally high performance greenhouses, having 30 square meters the 40 foot one and 15 square meters the 20 foot one, and 4 floors, making a total of 120 square meters of culture, growing sweet potatoes and strawberries.

20ft: 15 sqm / 150 sqft occupied area totaling 60 sqm / 600 sqft area for growing strawberries and sweet potatoes.

40ft: 30 sqm / 300 sqft occupied area totaling 120 sqm / 1200 sqft area for growing strawberries and sweet potatoes.

Each machine has a robot inside that waters, plants and harvests the sweet potatoes and strawberries in precise time. The robot harvests a row of ten plants each day, planting new ones on the same local area.

The robot takes the harvested sweet potatoes to the oven placed on the front of the machine and roast them during the day, starting at 9:00h, then at each hour, the robot take the roasted sweet potatoes and put another set of up to 7 on the oven, 6 times, 6 hours roasting sweet potatoes, roasting all of the harvested sweet potatoes of the day until about 15:00h.

The power comes from solar panels and from a wind turbine which are placed on the roof of the container, after the container is parked.

They are greenhouses that produce sweet potatoes with an automatic system that roasts the potatoes, ready for consumption.

People buy potatoes directly from the machines which are the producer, thus reducing the ecological footprint of transport.

Method to fight hunger:

The machines work well if they are spread out as close to the agglomerations of starving people as possible, they are left where a semi-trailer can go.

Each machine is transported only once to the site, which then produces minimally enough food for 20 people every day.

Each machine is good for places with climatic changes since it can withstand up to 8 months of drought. Compatible with the Madagascar climate.

Maintenance

This 40ft container machine only has to be sent once, and it will be producing food everyday and only have maintenance every 10 years with a pack of substituting batteries and grow leds, as that material has this wear, and refills the fertilizer silo.
